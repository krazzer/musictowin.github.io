Minoes

An old girl now, but still today,
she makes us smile, in every way.
Sometimes a fury, grabs a mouse
and brings it proudly to our house.

Refrein:
Minoes furry friend
Streetwise and bright
Knows how to make a stand
In love or fight

We know each other so very well
She blinks her eyes and you can tell.
A gliding feather, found by chance,
makes her perform an ecstatic dance.

Refrein:

Meeting an enemy in her domain;
might want your help to fight it again.
But when she's tired, she jumps on my lap
Taking her time for a purrrfull nap.
