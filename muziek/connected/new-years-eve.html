New Years Eve

In the warmth of the fire,
having a mindflow.
I see pictures of my past,
how did my life go.
Balancing the ups and downs,
where to refer to.
Any cause to change my ways,
something I should do.

And before the open fireplace,
you put a log onto the glow.
Eyes reflecting thousand sparkles,
Then suddenly I know.

Greatest aim is for
plain posession.
They use botox
against age.
And fight battles for
social standing.
What about their
mental stage.

It's about such simple virtues,
like integrity and truth,
like forgiving and attention.
Not about eternal youth.

Close the one year,
start another.
Come and have
a glass of wine.
Thinking of my
good intentions.
But most of all,
we're fine.

As for you, my life companion,
free of thought, creative mind.
Be fanatic, loving, caring.
Don't ever change, one of a kind.
Be fanatic, loving, caring.
Don't ever change, one of a kind.
