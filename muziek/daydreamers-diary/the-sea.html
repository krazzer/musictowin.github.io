The Sea

When my mind is filled with sorrow
I like to walk along the shore

Seems any problem for tomorrow
Don't give me worries anymore

Heading up against the seawind
A taste of salt is in the air

And screaming seagulls quickly fleeing
Before your footsteps bring you there

The lonesome fisherman is reeling
And tries to haul another prey

This offers me a way of dealing
With all the stress during the day

Just now the waves are growing stronger
Wandering comes to an end

I know I should have stayed here longer
The sea wil always be my friend.


Tekst en muziek: Ton van der Winden
