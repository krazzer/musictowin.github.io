Faces By HER

It’s not every kind of art
that gets into my heart.
But this one does, I have to say
Where do these people link
What did they do or think
Whatever part they used to play

Faces by H.E.R.
What was their goal
In still or stir
Once played their role

Imaginings in brown,
faces in smile or frown,
inviting me to step ahead
and take a closer look
in this enormous book
An endless fascinating view.

Faces by H.E.R.
What was their goal
In still or stir
Once played their role

H.E.R. in the artist way
shows people sad or gay.
Distress or glamour, high or low.
Together in this frame
people without a name
will live forever as they go.
